# Building With Intention

The best web work starts by making the next action obvious. Before a framework,
animation, or integration enters the conversation, I like to ask what the page
needs to help someone understand, decide, or move forward.

That question keeps the work grounded. It makes performance practical, copy more
direct, and interfaces calmer. A portfolio, landing page, or campaign page can
still have personality, but the personality should support the job of the page.

## A Small Checklist

- Make the primary message visible without hunting.
- Keep layouts predictable enough to scan quickly.
- Give every interaction a clear result.
- Remove anything that only proves the builder was busy.

When the fundamentals are strong, the polish feels earned.
